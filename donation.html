OUR CONTRIBUTION TO THE SOCIETY:

During this difficult time, we provided:

  • Essential Safety Kits: We distributed safety kits, including masks, sanitizers, and basic medical supplies to help protect our children and their families from the spread of the virus.
  • Rice Bag & Grocery Donations: Understanding the financial strain many families faced, we donated rice bags and groceries to households with special children, ensuring they had the nourishment needed to get through the crisis.

These efforts have made a real difference in the lives of many special children and their families, allowing them to face the challenges of the pandemic with a sense of security and hope. Our mission is to continue this support, but we can't do it alone.

  • Nutrition and Well-being: Food: Nutritious meals are essential for the physical and mental development of the children. Your donation will ensure they receive balanced diets, promoting their overall well-being, growth, and energy levels, enabling them to engage more actively in learning and therapy sessions.
  • Dignity and Comfort: Clothing: Providing appropriate clothing fosters a sense of dignity and belonging among the children. Comfortable, well-fitting clothing enhances their self-esteem and ensures they feel cared for, improving their emotional well-being.
  • Health and Longevity: Medicine: Many children at the school require regular medical care, and your donation towards medicine ensures they receive the necessary treatment and health support. This helps manage their conditions, reduces illness, and enables them to lead more active lives.
  • Holistic Development: Therapy: Access to therapies such as physiotherapy, occupational therapy, and speech therapy is essential for the development of intellectually disabled children. By donating towards therapy, you are enabling children to improve their motor skills, communication abilities, and overall functional independence, giving them the tools to engage with the world around them.
  • Empowerment through Skills: Vocational Training Kits: Vocational training equips children with skills that are essential for future employment and self-reliance. Your donation of training kits will provide hands-on learning experiences, allowing children to develop job-related skills that can offer them a sense of purpose and financial independence later in life. This is especially important for older children who are transitioning into adulthood.
