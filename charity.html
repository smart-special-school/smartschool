HOME FOR ORPHAN

The home for intellectually disabled orphans at the Smart Special School provides a safe and nurturing environment for children who have no family to care for them. It offers shelter, education, therapy, nutritious meals, and medical care, ensuring these vulnerable children receive the support they need to thrive. Along with daily essentials, the home fosters emotional well-being and personal growth, giving each child the opportunity to develop essential life skills and independence. Through specialized attention and care, the home offers these children a sense of belonging, dignity, and hope for a better future.
