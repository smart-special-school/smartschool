EMPOWERING ABILITIES,TRANSFORMING LIVES!

The Shine Mentally Challenged and Rehabilitation Trust was established in 2003 at Arakkonam, Ranipet district, Tamilnadu in response to the growing need for specialized care and education for individuals with intellectual disabilities.

It started with only two children and very basic amenities. Despite the initial struggles and minimal resources, the school gradually grew, serving more children from Arakkonam and the surrounding areas. The school not only focused on providing education but also created a nurturing environment where special children could thrive.

As the Trust evolved, it began to expand its services to meet the growing needs of the children. Recognizing the importance of nutrition, the school started providing three nutritious meals daily, along with two servings of milk and healthy snacks.

The need for a safe and secure environment became evident as the school encountered children who were denied shelter. This led to the establishment of a facility that offered not just education but also shelter, food, clothing, medicine, and vocational training to help these children become more self-reliant.

SHINE MENTALLY CHALLENGED AND REHABILITATION TRUST

Smart Special School is a non-governmental organization (NGO) dedicated to helping students with intellectual disabilities. Established in 2003, our mission is to provide a nurturing and supportive environment for these children, ensuring they receive integrated education along with essential therapies such as physiotherapy, occupational therapy, and speech therapy.

For over two decades, we have offered specialized care and education tailored to each childâ€™s needs, empowering them to achieve their full potential. Our school focuses on holistic development, offering services like:

  • Physiotherapy to improve motor skills and mobility
  • Occupational therapy to enhance daily living skills
  • Speech therapy to support communication and language development
  • Nutritious meals, including snacks and milk, ensuring overall health
  • Vocational training to prepare intellectually disabled adults for meaningful work

Despite our humble beginnings with only two children, we have now expanded to serve numerous families from Arakkonam and nearby regions. Our approach is not just educational but also therapeutic, focusing on the overall well-being of the children.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every child with intellectual disabilities is given the opportunity to learn, grow, and become self-reliant. Through vocational training, holistic care, and the provision of a safe and supportive space, we aim to create a future where these children are confident, empowered, and able to lead independent and fulfilling lives. Our vision is to expand our reach to support the 200+ children in the community who need education, therapy, and shelter, ensuring they all have a safe environment to thrive and achieve their full potential.

Our Mission

To empower intellectually disabled individuals through early intervention, integrated education, and holistic care. We believe in the transformative power of special education and appropriate therapies, as it plays a crucial role in maximizing a childâ€™s development and future independence. Our approach ensures that each child receives the necessary care, therapies, and training to thrive. we are committed to providing comprehensive support that caters to the well-being of each child and the community at large.

What we do

We also focus on training different categories of people to create a supportive environment for children with special needs, including:

  • Parents and caregivers, equipping them with the skills and knowledge necessary to support their childrenâ€™s development at home.
  • Teachers and special educators, ensuring they have the proper training to deliver individualized education and therapeutic interventions.
  • Healthcare professionals, to provide specialized therapies like physiotherapy, occupational therapy, and speech therapy with the utmost care and attention.
  • Community members, fostering awareness and inclusivity so that children with intellectual disabilities are accepted and supported in society.

Our services also include:

  • Vocational training, empowering children with life skills for a self-reliant future.
  • Personalized medical care and nutritious meals tailored to the needs of each child.
  • Safe and nurturing accommodations with dedicated caretakers for boys and girls.
  • Scholarships and stipends to ensure access to education for deserving students.
  • Family support services, including food, accommodation, and emotional care, to alleviate the challenges of caring for special children.

By providing early intervention and training various stakeholders, we aim to empower children with special needs to live independent and fulfilling lives, while creating a community that supports and nurtures them.

Join Us in Making a Difference

At Smart Special School, we believe that creating a supportive and inclusive environment for children with intellectual disabilities requires the involvement of the entire community. You can join us in our mission by contributing in various ways:

  • Volunteer your time: Help in classrooms, therapy sessions, or outreach programs aimed at educating parents and communities about the importance of early intervention and care for children with special needs.
  • Donate basic needs: Your contributions of food, medication, teaching aids, and other essentials directly impact the well-being of our students. Every donation helps us provide a nurturing environment for their growth and development.
