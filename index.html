about The School

Smart School, is a non-governmental organization established in 2003

We believe that every child deserves the chance to learn, grow, and shine in their own unique way. At Shine, we provide a nurturing and inclusive environment where education and therapy go hand in hand, offering comprehensive support through physiotherapy, occupational therapy, and speech therapy. Our aim is not only to teach, but to empower, fostering independence, confidence, and dignity in every child we serve.

For more than two decades, we have walked hand in hand with children and their families, delivering care that’s both compassionate and customized to each individual’s needs. Our approach to holistic development includes physiotherapy to improve motor skills and movement, occupational therapy to enhance daily living abilities, and speech therapy to strengthen communication and language. We also ensure the well-being of every student through nutritious meals, snacks, and milk provided daily. Beyond academics, we extend our care into adulthood through vocational training programs that equip individuals with practical skills, helping them find purpose and meaningful employment within society.

We Serve

Our care center

01

Early Intervention (0-6 Years)

Our Early Intervention Program focuses on identifying and nurturing developmental skills at a young age. With personalized therapies and play-based learning, we help children build strong foundations in movement, speech, and social interaction.

02

Day Care Centre (6-14 Years)

Our Day Care Centre offers a safe, structured, and engaging environment for school-aged children with intellectual disabilities. Each day blends learning, therapy, and play to promote growth, confidence, and social bonding.

03

Vocational Training

We provide hands-on vocational training to help older students learn useful life and work skills. From crafts to basic trades, we prepare them for greater independence and meaningful participation in the community.

04

Nutritious Food

Every child receives wholesome meals, snacks, and milk each day, ensuring balanced nutrition and good health. We believe that proper nourishment fuels both learning and happiness.

05

Scholarships and stipends

To ensure that every child has access to education and care, we offer scholarships and financial support. These programs ease the burden on families and help children continue their learning journey without interruption.

06

Family support services

We stand beside families through every step of their child’s development. Our support programs offer counseling, guidance, and workshops to help parents understand, accept, and empower their children with love and strength.

Therapies We Offer

We care each child

Physiotherapy

Helps children improve strength, balance, and mobility through guided physical exercises. Encourages independent movement and builds confidence in their daily activities.

Occupational Therapy

Focuses on developing fine motor skills and daily living abilities. Enables children to perform tasks like dressing, writing, and self-care with greater ease.

Speech Therapy

Enhances communication, speech clarity, and language understanding. Supports children in expressing themselves confidently and connecting with others.

Group Therapy

Encourages teamwork, sharing, and social interaction among peers. Builds friendship, empathy, and emotional connection in a safe, guided setting.

Play Therapy

Uses play as a natural way for children to express emotions and overcome challenges. Helps develop creativity, self-awareness, and emotional regulation through fun activities.

Pet Therapy

Introduces children to the calming presence of gentle animals. Promotes emotional healing, reduces anxiety, and nurtures kindness and empathy.

Applied Behaviour Analysis (ABA) Therapy

Uses structured methods to improve learning, behavior, and social skills. Encourages positive habits and independence through consistent guidance and reinforcement.
